We may collect, store and use the following kinds of personal data:

(a) information from your device and about your visits to and use of this App. We collect diagnostic, and personal information. This includes information about your activity such as your IP address, log files, website, performance logs, reports, crash, hardware model, operating system information, mobile network information, geographical location, browser information, referral source, length of visit and number of page views;

(b) information relating to any transactions carried out between you and us or other third parties processing your payment on or in relation to this App, including information relating to any purchases you make of our goods or services;

(c) information that you provide to us for the purpose of registering with us;

(d) information that you provide to us for the purpose of subscribing to our App services;

(e) Information about your account. We collect information such as your profile name and photo, online status and status messages, your contacts and receipts may be available to anyone who uses our App. You can use your App settings and the block feature in our App to manage the users of our App with whom you communicate and certain information you share;

(f) Any other information that you choose to send to us.
